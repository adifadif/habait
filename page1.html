Habait Enhanced
Audio-Visual Experience

See Jerusalem and the Temple come alive

Our Audio Visual guide is amazing, unique experience in its own right. We are using state of the art hardware to enable our visitors make the most out of their visit, as well as ground breaking mixed reality sound and visual experience, using cinematic productions shot in special studios.  


Your visit in the exhibition areas will be experienced to the full, when you will use our audio-visual guide, available in 11 different languages, while wearing special glasses.


In the beginning of your visit, you will be given a pair of smart Mixed Reality glasses. The lenses are transparent and bare no optical modifications. If you are already wearing your own optical glasses, the smart glasses can be worn on top. By wearing the smart glasses, you will be able to see and hear information and content that "exists" only to you and not in the real world. For instance, once you will set your eyes on the holy Menorah, you will hear the audio information related to it, through the speakers in your glasses. Simultaneously, you will then see a hologram of a priest approaching, attending the Menorah and lighting its lamps.
