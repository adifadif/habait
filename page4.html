FOOD & DRINK

Something for Everyone

Our Café & Restaurant specializes in contemporary and classic local cuisine according to seasonal character and nature's generosity. 

Great selection of drinks from "Old" and "New" world will satisfy most contrasting tastes searching for perfect drink to complement excellent food.

The Café & restaurant accommodates up to 80 simultaneous guests seated in two rooms. During the summer, enjoy the terrace with 28 seats.

A perfect place:

• to enjoy meals and drinks with family or friends;

• for business meetings and seminars;

• organising corporate events and presentations;

• for private meetings in our smaller room holding up to 18 guests;

• to celebrate closing of successful day with a glass of outstanding wine, coffee or any other drink of your choice.
