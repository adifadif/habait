About Habait

Habait is a touristic, cultural and educational center, at the heart of which is an accurate life-like reconstruction of Solomon’s Temple. To many, the most significant spiritual endeavour in Biblical times for everyone to experience and enjoy. The Museum is located in an atmospheric biblical landscape as the perfect time capsule.

You are welcome to relive history - walking all the way into the holy of holies through the court and the nave, accompanied by ground breaking Augmented Reality technology experience simulating the spiritual labor of the priests in their devotional duties. 

The Museum offers thematic exhibits, attractions, and cultural events as well as a restaurant & Café, a gift shop, and evening classes.

Whether you are a tourist, a student, an educator or a spiritual shepherd - Habait is a unique experience you can ill afford to miss.
