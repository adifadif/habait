GROUP VISITS

Groups are invited to explore Habait.
Request a guided tour or reserve admission and lead your own group.

Advance reservations are required for all adult and student groups of 5 or more, and for any third-party guided tours, regardless of size.

The benefits of every group reservation at Habait include:

- A dedicated arrival time slot for each group

- A dedicated check-in desk to bypass the general admissions line

- The ability to move through the galleries as a group

- Convenient coat and backpack storage 

Groups of Visitors with Disabilities

The Museum is committed to providing learning experiences

for Children, teens,  and adults of all abilities.

University, and Personal Groups

Tour Habait's temple and exhibitions with a private Museum guide or reserve group admission and bypass admission lines.

School Groups

Make Habait part of your school year! School groups may explore the museum with a guide, a teacher or independently.

Parish and Spiritual Groups

Explore Hanait's time capsule with your own shepherd. Assistance kits for spiritual leaders are available in many languages.
