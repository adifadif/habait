THE MAIN EXHIBIT

“For mine house shall be called an house of prayer for all people.” (Isaiah 56:7)

In the heart of Habait stands the main exhibit- an exact, full size replica of Solomon’s temple. This One of a kind reconstruction relies on the most recent archeological findings as well as historical and Midrash literature.


Visitors from around the world can now step into this time capsule, walk through its different parts, feel its stone walls and furnishings. The tour experience is enhanced with ground breaking Augmented Reality technologies that bring ceremonies and daily priestly toil to life, allowing visitors to immerse themselves in an atmosphere of antiquity and holiness like no other place.

The living spirit and universal vision of Habait reflects a core concept of the original Solomon’s temple as a center of tolerance and coexistence between different cultures and beliefs.
Ancient legends echo this concept in modern times, and emphasise the biblical portrayal of the temple as a house of prayer for all people.
